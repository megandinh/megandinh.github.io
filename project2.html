Event Poster.

Skills: Photoshop | Patience | Precision


I followed this tutorial produce my own event poster. In this case, I created a poster for Daylight Savings Time for Spring 2019. Since this time of the year always sneaks up on people, I figured that this poster would be a subtle and simple way to remind people to set their clocks an hour forward.

Although the steps are not too difficult, precision is important when adding layers, adjusting the masks, and considering color palettes. This project allowed me to become more comfortable with Photoshop tools, so I have a stronger understanding of how to use Photoshop and how to achieve certain outcomes.
