About.


Nice to meet you! I'm Megan, a third-year undergraduate majoring in Design and Interaction and minoring in Computer Science at UC San Diego.

My height ceases to grow beyond five feet, so I found alternative ways to grow besides chugging milk and chomping on calcium gummies. My secret is to try something new, write in a daily journal, take detours from plans, read books, and keep moving forward.

The culmination of my user research and interaction design experience was learning that I like making people feel recognized, represented, and empowered. I am inspired by random acts of kindness - opening a door for someone, giving compliments, exchanging smiles - that are intended to encourage people to think about others and try something new, and I intend on using design to deliver deliberate acts of kindess of the same intention.
