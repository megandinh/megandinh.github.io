Cinemagraph.

Skills: Photoshop | Patience


I followed this tutorial to create a cinemagraph of pouring milk. I practiced working with videos in Photoshop and manipulating them to produce a continuous motion of a specific subject. I also practiced masking surrounding objects on a video to emphasize motion on the subject of interest, which was the milk.

I learned that for best results, use a short video that has motion that is concentrated in a specific area. I made the mistake of trying to create a cinemagraph from a flower moving in the wind, but that flower had erratic movement that did not work well with cinemagraphs.
